About GVMI

Leading the future of maritime finance through innovation, expertise, and sustainable practices.

Company Overview

Golden Valley Marine Investment (GVMI) is Seacon Shipping Group's wholly-owned RFA platform, providing comprehensive maritime finance and asset management solutions to global stakeholders.

Founded on the principles of data-driven decision making and sustainable maritime practices, we have grown to become a trusted partner for shipping companies, financial institutions, and investors worldwide.

Our integrated approach combines cutting-edge market intelligence, innovative financing structures, and comprehensive risk management to deliver exceptional value across the entire maritime value chain.
